Introduction To Crochet

Crochet is an art form in which you use yarn and a crochet hook to create many things from toys to clothing. When you crochet something, you start off with either a magic ring, or a slipknot. Starting with a magic ring allows you to crochet 3-D items. Starting with a slip knot allows you to make 2-D items. When you make a magic ring, you have to start out by making stitches into the magic ring. To understand more, go to the helpful links that I have provided. You can continue making stitches until you have finished your crochet. When starting with a slipknot, you must first make chains. Then, you add crochets into those chains. For the next row of crochets, you must start with a chain to continue. For a more in depth explanation, watch the helpful links provided.
